Crew Orbital Docking Sim v0.6
by itsraineing

HOW TO PLAY
translate the capsule with the arrow keys, rotate with Q/E
move the capsule close to the ISS IDA
stay aligned (<10 degrees off) and move slowly (<1m/s)!
